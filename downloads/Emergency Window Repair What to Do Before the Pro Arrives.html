Common Window Emergencies & Immediate Actions

Broken Glass

Immediate Action: Clear area of people and pets, cover opening with plywood or heavy cardboard, and contact emergency service immediately.

Do NOT: Touch broken glass with bare hands or attempt to remove large pieces without proper tools.

Water Infiltration

Immediate Action: Place buckets and towels to catch water, identify source if possible, and remove standing water to prevent structural damage.

Do NOT: Ignore the problem or assume it will stop on its own—water damage escalates quickly.

Structural Failure

Immediate Action: Evacuate the immediate area if safe to do so, secure the area to prevent injury, and contact emergency services.

Do NOT: Attempt to repair structural issues yourself—this can cause further collapse or injury.
