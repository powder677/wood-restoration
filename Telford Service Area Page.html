Celebrating Telford's Unique Architectural Style

Telford is known for its charming streetscapes and beautifully preserved historic homes, particularly its collection of Craftsman and Victorian-era properties. The original wood doors on these homes are masterpieces of craftsmanship, featuring intricate details and high-quality materials that are simply not found in modern construction.

At Heritage Wood Restoration, we provide specialized door repair and restoration services for Telford homeowners. We have extensive experience working with the specific styles found in our community, ensuring that every repair honors the home's original design intent.

Our Telford Door Restoration Services Include:

  • Repair of sticking, sagging, or improperly aligned doors
  • Epoxy consolidation for rotted door frames and panels
  • Restoration of original hardware, including knobs, locks, and hinges
  • Repair and replacement of historic glass panels
  • Stripping and refinishing with period-appropriate materials
  • Weatherization to improve sealing and energy efficiency
