Our Mission

20 Years of Craftsmanship Dedicated to Wood Restoration

I've always been fascinated by architecture — the way lines, materials, and craftsmanship come together to create something timeless. For the past 20 years, I've kept my hands in the craft through custom woodworking, remodels, and furniture builds. From shaping intricate trim details to restoring antique pieces, I've always believed that great woodwork deserves to last for generations.

After a successful career running businesses for other people, I realized it was time to take the leap and put my skills, experience, and vision to work for myself. That's how Heritage Wood Restoration was born — a dedicated service focused on wood window epoxy reconstruction and restoration.

Why? Because too many homeowners are told their only option is to replace. In reality, many of these windows and doors can be repaired — not just patched, but structurally restored — for a fraction of the cost, all while preserving the character and craftsmanship that modern replacements just can't match.

Looking Ahead

Building a Model for Sustainable Craftsmanship

From the very beginning, I've envisioned this as more than just a one-man shop. With the right systems, training, and standards, wood window epoxy reconstruction can be scaled into a franchise model — bringing specialized restoration services to communities across the country.

Souderton and Telford are where it starts. The goal is simple: deliver top-tier craftsmanship, earn trust in every neighborhood we serve, and create a model that other skilled tradespeople can follow to build their own success stories.
