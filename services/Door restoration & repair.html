Our Door Restoration & Repair Process

Thorough Assessment

We begin with a comprehensive evaluation of your door's condition, examining wood integrity, hardware functionality, structural soundness, and operational issues. This allows us to develop a customized restoration plan that addresses all concerns.

Careful Removal & Documentation

Doors are carefully removed and thoroughly documented with detailed photos and measurements. This ensures proper reinstallation and helps identify any hidden issues that may require attention.

Structural Repairs

Damaged or deteriorated wood is selectively repaired or replaced using traditional joinery methods. We address warping, rot, and structural issues while preserving as much original material as possible.

Surface Preparation

Old finishes are carefully removed using appropriate methods that don't damage the underlying wood. All surfaces are prepared for new finishes while preserving original profiles and details.

Hardware Restoration

Original hardware is cleaned, repaired, and refinished where possible. Missing or damaged pieces are authentically reproduced to match period specifications and maintain historical accuracy.

Finishing & Protection

High-quality, historically appropriate finishes are applied using traditional techniques. This includes proper priming, staining (if desired), and topcoats that provide long-term protection.

Reinstallation & Adjustment

Restored doors are carefully reinstalled with proper weatherstripping and adjustments to ensure smooth operation, proper sealing, and optimal security.
