Sample Maintenance Tasks Included:

  • Inspect window operation and lubricate hardware
  • Check for drafts and air infiltration points
  • Examine paint condition and touch up as needed
  • Inspect weatherstripping and replace if necessary
  • Check for water infiltration and proper drainage
  • Examine wood condition for signs of rot or damage
  • Clean window tracks and channels
  • Inspect and maintain storm windows
