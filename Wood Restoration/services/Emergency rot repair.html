Our Emergency Rot Repair Process

Immediate Assessment

Our emergency response team arrives promptly to evaluate the extent of rot damage, identify safety concerns, and determine immediate stabilization needs.

Rapid Stabilization

Critical areas are immediately stabilized using temporary supports, moisture control, and emergency sealing to prevent further deterioration.

Precision Excavation

Soft, deteriorated wood is carefully removed while preserving sound material. This stops active rot and prepares the area for permanent repair.

Epoxy Consolidation

Advanced conservation-grade epoxy is applied to strengthen remaining sound wood and create a stable foundation for permanent repairs.

Structural Repairs

Damaged sections are replaced using traditional joinery methods and matching materials to restore full structural integrity.

Protective Finishing

Temporary protective finishes are applied to prevent further moisture intrusion while permanent finishing is scheduled.
