5 Signs Your Historic Windows Need Professional Restoration

Sticking or Hard-to-Operate Windows

Original windows that have become difficult to open or close often just need proper adjustment and restoration of their original hardware mechanisms.

Energy Efficiency Concerns

While old windows may feel drafty, restoration with proper weatherstripping and maintenance can significantly improve energy efficiency without losing authenticity.

Visible Paint Buildup

Multiple layers of paint can prevent windows from closing properly and trap moisture, leading to wood rot if not properly addressed through gentle stripping.

Rotting or Damaged Wood

Localized wood damage doesn't mean complete replacement is necessary. Expert restoration can repair and replace only the affected sections while maintaining original character.

Poor Curb Appeal

Faded paint, broken hardware, or foggy glass can make beautiful windows look unsightly. Professional restoration brings back their original elegance and charm.
